Code of Ethics

  • 1. Integrity and Honesty: All members of the academy are expected to uphold the highest standards of integrity, ensuring honesty in all academic and professional endeavors.
  • 2. Respect for Intellectual Property: The academy values and respects the intellectual property rights of others, emphasizing the importance of proper attribution and avoiding plagiarism.
  • 3. Privacy and Data Protection: Safeguarding personal and institutional data is paramount. Members must adhere to best practices in data privacy and security.
  • 4. Inclusivity and Diversity: The academy promotes an inclusive environment that respects and values diverse backgrounds, perspectives, and experiences.
  • 5. Accountability: Members are accountable for their actions and decisions, taking responsibility and addressing any misconduct appropriately.
  • 6. Professionalism: Maintaining a high level of professionalism in interactions, communications, and conduct is essential for fostering a positive learning environment.
  • 7. Commitment to Excellence: Striving for excellence in teaching, learning, and research is a core value, encouraging continuous improvement and innovation.
  • 8. Transparency: Open and transparent communication is encouraged to build trust and facilitate effective collaboration among all stakeholders.
  • 9. Ethical Use of Technology: Technology should be used responsibly and ethically, ensuring it enhances learning without causing harm or disruption.
  • 10. Continuous Learning and Development: The academy supports ongoing learning and professional development, encouraging members to stay updated with evolving best practices and ethical standards.
